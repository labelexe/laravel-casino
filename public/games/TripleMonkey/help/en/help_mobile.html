Triple Monkey TM

How to Play

Play mode and balance

The game can be played in both landscape and portrait mode on a mobile.

You can play the game in either FUN or REAL mode.

In fun mode, you are playing just for the fun of the score. In real mode, you are playing with money from your real money balance.

Your balance is shown in the BALANCE box both in fun and real mode.

Placing Bets

This game is played with a fixed number of active paylines. Your total bet per game round is your coin bet multiplied by the number of active paylines.

To select the bet value, use the BET OPTIONS control panel. The bet options panel displays the TOTAL BET amount staked per fixed playing lines (ways).

In portrait mode, you can open the panel by tapping on the BET OPTIONS button located below the reels and marked with '-' and '+'. Tapping the button again or tapping anywhere on the reels closes the panel.

In landscape mode, the panel can be opened by tapping the arrow button on the side of the screen. Tapping the arrow button again or tapping anywhere on the reels closes the panel.

To set the bet amount, use the '-' and '+' buttons.

Autoplay Mode

Hold the SPIN button to open the AUTOPLAY settings panel. Tapping the arrow button or anywhere on the reels closes the panel.

Turbo Mode

When TURBO Mode is on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster.

Tapping the TURBO button switches Turbo Mode on or off.

In portrait mode, the TURBO button is located below the reels. In landscape mode, the button can be opened by tapping the arrow button on the side of the screen. Tapping the arrow button again or tapping anywhere on the reels closes the panel.

PAYTABLE

Payouts

Winning combinations start from the leftmost reel and the symbols have to be consecutive along a payline.

Regular wins are multiplied by the COIN BET.

Only the highest win pays on each payline.

Coinciding wins on different paylines are added.

The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information see the TERMS AND CONDITIONS section.

RESPINS

During RESPINS the reels are spun automatically using the same COIN BET as per the spin that won the RESPINS.

Disconnection Policy

Note on disconnection

If you are disconnected from the Internet in the middle of the game, log in to the casino again. Once you have reopened the game, you will be able to continue playing from the point where the game was disconnected.

Note on malfunctions

Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
